My Favourite Parts Of the Game

I began playing League of Legends about 6 years ago now.

A friend of mine introduced me to it, and within a few short weeks, I was hooked.

I think the reason I love the game so much, is there are endless combinations.

There are currently over 140 characters to pick from, and many different items you can build in the game to become stronger. Games can be similar, but never quite the same.

Since it is an online game, I often call friends (especially friends who live far away) and play with them.
